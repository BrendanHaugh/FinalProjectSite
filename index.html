Home

Hello! My name is Brendan Haugh and I'm a student in Wake Tech's Graphic Design Program. I dabble in illustration, photography, and other forms of art. This site contains some samples of my illustration and photography work, as well as a contact form and links to my social media channels. Thanks for stopping by.

ILLUSTRATION

Above is a selection of artwork created using Clip Studio Paint. I primarily illustrate in a cartoony artstyle with bright colors and exaggerated features. I have created several pieces featuring an original character of mine, Comette, as well as artwork featuring mermaids in different underwater scenarios. I hope you find the above images fun and enjoyable!

PHOTOGRAPHY

This is a selection of photos I've taken over the years with my Canon brand camera. I enjoy taking photos of nature, landscapes, and food. I put an emphasis on capturing the texture and detail of whatever I photograph. I'll often spend several minutes trying to get the right shot with the proper focus and aperture. I hope my effort shows through in this selection.
